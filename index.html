About

Perfumes are a blend of aromatic oils and ingredients designed to create pleasant and memorable scents. They come in various concentrations, such as Eau de Parfum or Eau de Toilette, offering different levels of intensity. Fragrances are often categorized by notes, with top, heart, and base notes unfolding at different stages after application. Perfumes are deeply personal and can evoke emotions, memories, or a sense of confidence. They can be floral, woody, citrus, or musky, offering a wide range of options for different preferences and occasions.
